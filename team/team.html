FREIMARKT

Time banking system

Timebank FREIMARKT is an overall timebank targeting whole society, yet focusing also at INTEGRATING and INCLUDING some specific target groups, which are otherwise difficult to include on the € job market. Those specific target groups are amongst others:
• Immigrants
• Unemployed youth • People with physical or psychical special needs
• People just coming from a period of therapy (p.ex. because of substance-abuse,…)
• Elders who lost their job or else are in pension yet still fit to work/contribute
